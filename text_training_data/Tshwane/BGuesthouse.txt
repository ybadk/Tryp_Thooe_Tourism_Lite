B Guest House
Come and feel right at home in the rich ambiance of this charming Guesthouse in Arcadia PretoriaTshwane The main residence was built in Dutch style in 1929 by architect builder and painter Henk Horstmanshof and declared a Provincial Heritage site The house is characterized by the wellpreserved original stained glass front door and windows and its unique architecture An environment of warmth and charm is created with friendly hospitality and personal attention
