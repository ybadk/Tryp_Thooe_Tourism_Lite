Ditsong Kruger Museum
The Museum consists of the original house in which SJP Kruger President of the old ZuidAfrikaansche Republiek ZAR and his family lived during the last years of the 19th century as well as two display halls and President Krugers State Railway Coach The Kruger house built in 1884 was the original home of Paul Kruger President of the former Transvaal Boer Republic The house has been refurbished to reflect the time when the President and his wife Gezina Kruger lived there
