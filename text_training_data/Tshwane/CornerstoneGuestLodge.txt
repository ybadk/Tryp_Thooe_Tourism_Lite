Cornerstone Guest Lodge
Cornerstone Lodge is situated in a very central area in the east of Pretoria and although we are close to all the main attractions in and around Pretoria you can often hear the quiet of the night The original house was built in the early 1970s and then purchased by us in 1997 At first it was just a dream to convert our home into a guesthouse but it soon became a reality We were able to start with the project to change our home into a great guesthouse
